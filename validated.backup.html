Our Process

Things We Do

01

Strategy

We start by understanding your channel's goals, audience, and competitive landscape. Every decision is rooted in a clear strategic direction built for your brand.

02

Ideation

We generate a steady pipeline of video ideas at the intersection of what your audience craves and what your brand stands for — concepts designed to travel.

03

Packaging

A great idea lives or dies on its packaging. We craft titles and thumbnails that earn the click — headline precision with visual contrast that stops the scroll.

04

Scripting

We map out the emotional arc of every video — the hook, the tension, the payoff. Our beat sheets give creators a roadmap that keeps watch time high from first frame to last.

05

Edit Reviews

We review every single edit to ensure maximum retention — checking pacing, hooks, and structure so each video performs at its full potential.

06

Data Analysis

At the end of each month we do a full post-mortem of what worked and what didn't, adjusting strategy going forward based on real performance data.
